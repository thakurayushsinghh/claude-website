Who We Are

Rooted in Trust,
Built for Growth

Founded in 2009, Shree Nidhi Capitals was born from a simple belief: every individual and business deserves access to fair, transparent, and timely financial support. Over 15 years, we have grown from a single branch to a trusted name serving thousands of families and entrepreneurs across Gujarat and beyond.

We combine the warmth of a community lender with the professionalism of a modern financial institution — offering a full suite of loan products designed around your unique life goals, not just your credit score.
